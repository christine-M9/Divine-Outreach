Divine Outreach 2026 Mentorship Program – Kibera Bible Club

In 2026, Divine Outreach continues its mission of raising a generation grounded in faith, character, and purpose through the Mentorship Program at Kibera Bible Club, running throughout the year until November.

This initiative, carried out in partnership with Love Acts 4 Africa, is dedicated to guiding children and young people by providing spiritual mentorship, life guidance, and encouragement to help them grow with confidence and hope.

Through regular mentorship sessions, interactive activities, and biblical teachings, the program nurtures values such as integrity, resilience, compassion, and leadership.

The mentorship program creates a safe and supportive environment where children can learn, ask questions, and be inspired to pursue a better future despite the challenges around them. Together, Divine Outreach and Love Acts 4 Africa are committed to empowering these young lives spiritually, emotionally, and socially.

As the program continues throughout the year, both organizations remain devoted to walking alongside these children—offering guidance, encouragement, and the message of hope that every child deserves the opportunity to grow, dream, and thrive.
